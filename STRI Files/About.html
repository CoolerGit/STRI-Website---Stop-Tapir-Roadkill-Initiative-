Stop Tapir Roadkill Initiative

AIMS

EDUCATE

Emphasise that roadkill is a global issue with an increasing number of mammals, birds, reptiles, and amphibians being hit by speeding vehicles.

AWAKEN

Make citizens sympathise towards animals and realise that roadkill threatens a massive amount of wildlife, which can lead to the loss of population density and endangerment.

PROTECT

STRI, along with worldwide netizens, will work towards minimising the number of Malayan Tapir casualties due to roadkill accidents. Our ultimate goal is to restore its IUCN status from endangered to safety.
